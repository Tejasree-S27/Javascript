Monday is considered the start of the workweek in many cultures.

Tuesday is named after the Norse god Tyr, associated with war and justice.

Wednesday is named after Odin, the chief Norse god.

Thursday is named after Thor, the Norse god of thunder.

Friday is named after Frigg, the Norse goddess of love and beauty.

Saturday is named after Saturn, the Roman god of agriculture.

Sunday is traditionally a day of rest and worship in many cultures.
